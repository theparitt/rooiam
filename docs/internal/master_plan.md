## Current product direction

Rooiam is the self-hosted passwordless IAM for multi-tenant SaaS.

That means the near-term plan should optimize for:

- self-host trust
- passwordless auth quality
- multi-tenant workspace control
- hosted login and OIDC integration
- clear operator and tenant workflows
